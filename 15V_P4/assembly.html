Description

This assembly shows assembly of Saccharomyces cerevisiae strain 15V-P4 and, a part of the Peterhof genetic collection

Please refer to NCBI BioProject [to be released].

Methods

Contigs (see ../15V_contigs) were assembled with chromosomer (https://github.com/gtamazian/chromosomer/) using S288C genome R64-1-1 (http://downloads.yeastgenome.org/sequence/S288C_reference/genome_releases/S288C_reference_genome_R64-1-1_20110203.tgz) as reference.
